"We were somewhere around Barstow on the edge of the desert when the drugs began 
to take hold. I remember saying something like 'I feel a bit lightheaded; maybe you 
should drive.' And suddenly there was a terrible roar all around us, and the sky was full 
of what looked like huge bats, all swooping and screeching and diving around the car, 
which was going about a hundred miles an hour with the top down to Las Vegas. And a 
voice was screaming: 'Holy Jesus! What are these goddamn animals?'"
